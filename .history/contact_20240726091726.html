What is MCC

Mumbai Coding Club offers a structured, flexible and guided learning program for software engineers to accelerate their career. This program is open for all software engineers irrespective of the 1.5 years of work experience or their current place of work.

Leveraging our extensive network of IT professionals, we have access to a diverse talent pool comprising developers, engineers, analysts, project managers, and more. Whether you need temporary staff for a specific project or to cover peak workloads, we have the resources to meet your needs.

We recognize the importance of agility and flexibility in today's fast-paced business environment. Our contract staffing solutions allow you to scale your workforce up or down quickly in response to changing business demands, without the long-term commitment of permanent hires.

Contract staffing offers a cost-effective solution for managing fluctuating workloads and specialized projects. By hiring contract professionals on a temporary basis, you can avoid the overhead costs associated with full-time employees, such as benefits, training, and severance.

Our streamlined recruitment process ensures a seamless experience from job requisition to candidate placement. We handle all aspects of the staffing process, including sourcing, screening, interviewing, onboarding, and payroll administration, allowing you to focus on your core business priorities.
